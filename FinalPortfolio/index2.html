- ABOUT US -

"Hey, it’s Romhel. Romhelson Garfin. Don’t adjust your… whatever device you’re reading this on. It's me, live and on the internet. No return engagements, no encore, and, of course, absolutely no requests. Get a snack. Settle in. Because I’m about to tell you about myself. More specifically, why I pursue IT. And if you’re reading this website, here are the reasons why."

This is Romhelson Garfin. I was born and raised in the Bicol Region, where I also attended college. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. Because I desire to work in an IT-related sector and can picture myself doing so, I traveled to Metro Manila to hunt for IT employment such as tech assistance, web development, Java programming, or even QA. Finding bugs truly tests my programming skills, even if it can be frustrating at times, especially when you can't find them or debug them, but I know that it's worth it to get the software to run. When I was younger, I first became interested in IT after watching my cousin create simple games and other things. I then told myself that I would one day pursue a career in IT.

About myself, I consider myself an introvert (INFJ or INFP). I interact with other people; I'm not a snob either, but I don't know how to approach someone to start a conversation, and most of the time it is such awkward communication. I only talk a lot around my close friends or someone I am comfortable with.

In terms of my hobbies, I love playing indoor games such as card games, board games, puzzle games, and finding objects or solving crime games. I sometimes draw, but just to pass the time. I also sing, but only when I am alone.
